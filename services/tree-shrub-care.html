Tree & Shrub Care

Pruning, removal, and health care to keep your trees and shrubs beautiful and safe.

Schedule this service

Overview

Proper tree and shrub care is essential for safety, health, and aesthetics. Our certified arborists provide pruning, trimming, removal, stump grinding, and disease management. We use industry best practices to promote strong growth and prevent damage. Whether you need a single tree pruned or an entire lot cleared, we have the equipment and expertise.

What's included

  • Improved tree health and structure
  • Enhanced safety by removing hazardous limbs
  • Better sunlight and airflow for your landscape
  • Expert diagnosis and treatment of diseases

How it works

1

Inspection & Assessment

We evaluate the health, structure, and risks of your trees and shrubs.

2

Pruning or Removal Plan

Common questions

When is the best time to prune trees?

Late winter or early spring is ideal for most trees, but we can prune at any time for safety reasons.

Do you remove stumps after tree removal?

Yes, we offer stump grinding to remove the stump below ground level, allowing you to replant or sod.
